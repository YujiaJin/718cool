    NYC Base Animal Shelter

    Every year in this country, more than 4 million dogs enter shelters. Fewer than half of them will survive. Millions more wander alone, abused, hurt, and hungry. For too many of these wonderful animal companions, the end comes without ever knowing the love and dignity they deserve. By working together, we can solve this problem.

    Adopt – Volunteer – Donate is our motto. We encourage you to adopt your pets from local shelters and rescues. Then, volunteer your time to help them. Just a small commitment from you will make a big difference in the lives of dogs waiting for their forever homes. Finally, donate. No gift is too small to make a difference. The PEDIGREE Foundation supports the people and organizations that work to ensure better outcomes for all dogs in need.
